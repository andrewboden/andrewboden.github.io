DITCIT

Spring 2020

Datagram in the CIT (DITCIT) is a private hackathon targeted for athletes at Brown. With an ever-growing role in our lives, technology is often taken for granted. We use it everyday but often don't think about what's going on behind the screens. Classes in the CS department look great but can often be seen as scary rather than fun. Datagram aims to makes the area of computer science less intimidating amung the athletic community while celebrating technology.
